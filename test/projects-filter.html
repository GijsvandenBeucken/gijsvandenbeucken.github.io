  • van Gogh

    This recycle-based artwork is depicting the girl from The Potato Eaters by Vincent van Gogh. ... I used waste material to construct the artwork.
    It contains waste material, packaging and discarded products from our modern society like an old i-phone (can you spot it?), an lp of una paloma blanco etc. The artwork measures two by two metres.
    The work was exhibited by the van Gogh museum in Amsterdam. Press

    “The recycle-portraits of Vincent van Goghs Potato Eaters show the urgent matters and challenges concerning reducing plastic waste, the importance of recycling and sustainability through the eyes of a historic masterpiece."
